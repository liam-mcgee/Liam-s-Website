Custom Fidget Spinner

For this project my class was challenged to design a 3-D model of a fidget spinner on the website Tinker-Cad so it could then be cut from wood using our state of the art laser cutter. It was difficult with the design constraints of a minimum width per segment and selective materials. Each group member had an extra challenge such as budgeting or managing the laser printer however my challenge was to design caps for finger holds on the device. I had fun during this project and found it cool how I could use different materials to ultimately come together for a finished product.
